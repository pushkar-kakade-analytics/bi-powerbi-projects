# Financial MIS Dashboard – KPI Framework

## Overview
The Financial MIS Dashboard is designed to provide executive leadership and business stakeholders with centralized visibility into financial and operational performance.

The KPI framework focuses on revenue monitoring, profitability tracking, cashflow management, and project performance analysis.

---

# Core Financial KPIs

## 1. Revenue Actual

### Definition
Actual revenue recognized for a project during the reporting month.

### Purpose
Measures realized business performance.

### Business Importance
Used to evaluate project revenue growth and financial achievement.

---

## 2. Revenue AOP

### Definition
Planned or budgeted revenue target for the reporting period.

### Purpose
Represents expected financial performance.

### Business Importance
Used for target comparison and performance monitoring.

---

## 3. Variance

### Definition
Difference between Actual Revenue and AOP Revenue.

### Formula
Revenue Actual - Revenue AOP

### Purpose
Measures overperformance or underperformance.

### Business Importance
Helps leadership identify business gaps and performance deviations.

---

## 4. Variance %

### Definition
Percentage difference between Actual and AOP Revenue.

### Formula
(Variance / Revenue AOP) * 100

### Purpose
Measures performance efficiency against planned targets.

### Business Importance
Used in executive performance reviews.

---

## 5. Project Cost

### Definition
Total execution and operational cost incurred for the project.

### Purpose
Tracks project spending and cost management.

### Business Importance
Critical for profitability analysis.

---

## 6. Profit

### Definition
Difference between Revenue Actual and Project Cost.

### Formula
Revenue Actual - Project Cost

### Purpose
Measures project profitability.

### Business Importance
Used to evaluate financial health of projects.

---

## 7. Profit Margin %

### Definition
Profit percentage against revenue.

### Formula
(Profit / Revenue Actual) * 100

### Purpose
Measures operational profitability efficiency.

### Business Importance
Used to compare project financial performance.

---

# Billing & Collection KPIs

## 8. Billing

### Definition
Total invoice value raised during the reporting period.

### Purpose
Tracks billing activity and revenue realization process.

### Business Importance
Helps monitor project invoicing performance.

---

## 9. Collection

### Definition
Actual payment received from customers.

### Purpose
Tracks customer payment realization.

### Business Importance
Critical for working capital and cashflow management.

---

## 10. Collection Efficiency %

### Definition
Percentage of billing successfully collected.

### Formula
(Collection / Billing) * 100

### Purpose
Measures collection effectiveness.

### Business Importance
Used to monitor customer payment recovery performance.

---

# Cashflow KPIs

## 11. Cash Inflow

### Definition
Total incoming cash during the reporting period.

### Purpose
Tracks incoming business cash movement.

### Business Importance
Supports liquidity monitoring.

---

## 12. Cash Outflow

### Definition
Total outgoing payments during the reporting period.

### Purpose
Tracks operational and vendor payments.

### Business Importance
Used for cashflow management and cost monitoring.

---

## 13. Net Cashflow

### Definition
Difference between Cash Inflow and Cash Outflow.

### Formula
Cash Inflow - Cash Outflow

### Purpose
Measures net cash movement.

### Business Importance
Critical for financial planning and liquidity management.

---

# Working Capital KPIs

## 14. Accounts Receivable (AR)

### Definition
Outstanding customer payments pending collection.

### Purpose
Tracks customer receivables.

### Business Importance
Used to monitor delayed collections and customer payment risk.

---

## 15. Accounts Payable (AP)

### Definition
Outstanding vendor payments pending settlement.

### Purpose
Tracks vendor liabilities.

### Business Importance
Used for vendor payment planning and working capital management.

---

# Time Intelligence KPIs

## 16. Revenue YTD

### Definition
Cumulative revenue from start of financial year until reporting month.

### Purpose
Tracks annual business performance.

### Business Importance
Used for executive financial reviews.

---

## 17. Revenue MTD

### Definition
Revenue generated during the current reporting month.

### Purpose
Tracks monthly operational performance.

### Business Importance
Used for periodic business monitoring.

---

- Profitability tracking
- Cashflow monitoring
- Working capital analysis
- Trend analysis
- Project performance monitoring
